My Connection to UMD & Lake Superior

The University of Minnesota Duluth is a great choice for any business student and a great campus for local members of the community. Both my mother and brother have graduated (or are on the path to graduate) at UMD, which has helped me visualize myself on the campus.

Another reason for choosing UMD is its location next to Lake Superior. I love being able to drive by the beautiful scenery from the lake and the lift bridge every day. The shops in Canal Park are very fun to visit, and the Bentleyville lights display is truly one of a kind here in Duluth.

A great thing about being here at UMD is the opportunity to grow toward whatever path you choose. While doing so I have met a lot of new people and can occasionally meet my brother for lunch. I am excited for what the University of Minnesota Duluth has to offer in these four years of education!
