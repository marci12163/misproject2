Photography

One of my favorite activities outside of school and work is photography. I got my first camera at the young age of nine and I have fell in love with the hobby since. Something I am very proud of is how I submitted my photos at age ten to a newspaper contest and all three photos made it in the local newspaper! I also took my brother's graduation photos as well as one of my friends. One thing about me is whenever I have a chance, I love to be outside taking pictures!

Listening to Music

There are so many talented music artist out there and I am constantly finding new people to listen too. Something about playing music, especially with others, is something I really love.
